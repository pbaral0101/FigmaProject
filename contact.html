Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability — contact our concierge in advance.

Is airport pickup complimentary?

Complimentary airport transfers are included for Presidential Villa guests. All other room categories can arrange transfers at a fee — please book at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you cater to dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Our kitchen team accommodates all dietary needs including vegan, gluten-free, halal, and severe allergy protocols. Please inform us at the time of booking.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 72+ hours before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48–72 hours are charged 50% of the first night. No-shows are charged in full.
