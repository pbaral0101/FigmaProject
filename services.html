Resort Services

Everything you need for a perfect stay — all under one roof, available around the clock.

What We Offer

Six Pillars of Novara Living

Our services are designed to anticipate every need — before you even ask.

🍽️

Fine Dining Restaurant

Our three on-site restaurants serve international cuisine alongside authentic Nepali favourites. The Lakeside Terrace offers candlelit dinners with mountain views — a setting as memorable as the food itself. Breakfast buffet daily 6:30–10:30 AM; à la carte all day.

Open: 6:30 AM – 11:00 PM
🌿

Spa & Wellness Centre

Rejuvenate at Novara Spa — 1,800 m² of pure tranquility. Choose from signature Himalayan stone massages, Ayurvedic treatments, aromatherapy wraps, and hydrotherapy circuits. Our certified therapists craft every session to your personal wellness goals.

Open: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM · Advance booking recommended
🏊

Infinity Swimming Pool

Our 50-metre heated infinity pool appears to merge seamlessly with Phewa Lake below. A separate children's pool and a heated adults-only plunge pool are also available. Towels, sunbeds, and poolside cocktail service included for all guests.

Open: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
📶

High-Speed Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected everywhere on the estate with our resort-wide gigabit fibre network. Separate high-bandwidth conference connections available for business guests. Secure, private VPN-friendly access throughout all rooms and common areas.

Available: 24/7 · No usage limits
🚗

Airport Pickup & Transfer

Your journey to Novara begins the moment you land. Our fleet of luxury vehicles — including electric SUVs — meets you at Pokhara Regional Airport with a personalised welcome. Transfers to Kathmandu (Tribhuvan Airport) also available on request.

Book 48 hrs in advance · Complimentary for Presidential Villa guests
🛎️

24/7 Room Service

Whatever you need, whenever you need it. Our in-room dining menu spans 60 dishes from midnight snacks to full gourmet breakfasts. Same-day laundry, turndown service with locally-sourced chocolates, and a nightly wellness amenity are also included.

Available: 24 hours · 7 days a week
