Our Story

Novara Resort

Founded in 2008  ·  Pokhara, Nepal

Novara Resort was born from a single conviction: that true luxury is not about extravagance alone, but about the feeling of being genuinely cared for. Nestled at the edge of Phewa Lake against the backdrop of the Annapurna range, our 42-acre estate was designed to disappear into the landscape rather than overpower it.

Over the past 16 years we have welcomed guests from 78 countries, earned three consecutive World Luxury Hotel Awards, and — most importantly — built a team of 200 dedicated hospitality professionals who treat every arrival as a homecoming.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To create an immersive sanctuary where guests experience the finest in personalised hospitality, sustainable luxury, and cultural connection — leaving every visitor restored, inspired, and eager to return.

🌿 Our Vision

To be South Asia's most celebrated eco-luxury resort — a place where timeless natural beauty and world-class service coexist in perfect harmony, inspiring a new standard for responsible, heartfelt hospitality.

💛 Our Values

Genuine warmth · Sustainable stewardship · Relentless attention to detail · Cultural authenticity · Continuous innovation in the guest experience.

♻️ Sustainability

We run on 60% solar energy, source 80% of ingredients from local farms, and have eliminated single-use plastics resort-wide since 2019 — because a pristine natural world is our most valuable asset.
